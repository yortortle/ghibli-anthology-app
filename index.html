Studio Ghibli Anthology

This project is designed to provide an accurate and consise anthology of the works of Studio Ghibli. Studio Ghibli is an animation sudio based in Japan known for its expansive worldbuilding, fantasy lands, and storytelling. The films produced by them are widely known and recognized, and many have heard of films such as Spiried Away or Princess Mononoke. Please use the buttons below to display either all of Studio Ghiblis films by date, or filter them by specific director.

Director Importance:

The reason sorting by director is important is because of the massive influence that Hayao Miyazaki actually had on these films. Some diehard viewers of the Ghibli films mainly consider the ones directed by the elder Miyazaki to be important, and others to be tertiary. This isn't to say the others aren't good, films such as Porco Rosso and Return of The Cat are great films, but the cultural impact they've had is minimal compared to films such as Princess Mononoke and Spirited away.
